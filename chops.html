Description

This is a favorite recipe that we make at home all the time.

It's fast and easy to make, you can make these over white rice or some great garlic mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 stick butter
  • 4 (1/2-inch thick) pork chops
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse ground pepper
  • 3 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh garlic

Directions

  1. Melt butter in 12-inch skillet until sizzling; add pork chops.
  2. Cook over medium-high heat, turning once, 8-10 minutes or until browned.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Remove chops to serving platter. Keep warm.
  4. Place onions in skillet with pan juices; sprinkle with rosemary.
  5. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 8-10 minutes or until onions are caramelized.
  6. Stir in garlic; continue cooking 2-3 minutes or until garlic is softened.
  7. Return chops to pan; continue cooking 4-5 minutes or until pork reaches at least 145Â°F and is no longer pink.
  8. Place over rice or mashed potatoes.
